# New York City Congressional Districts

Simple feature collection of NYC Congressional Districts. EPSG:2263,
NAD83 / New York Long Island (ftUS).

## Usage

``` r
nyc_congressional_districts_sf
```

## Format

### `nyc_congressional_districts_sf`

A simple feature collection with 13 rows and 4 columns:

- cong_dist:

  Congressional district number

- shape_leng:

  Shape length

- shape_area:

  Shape area

- geometry:

  Multipolygon

## Source

<https://www.nyc.gov/content/planning/pages/resources#datasets>

## Details

Boundaries of NYC Congressional Districts. Produced by NYC Planning
Department. Release: 25C, August 2025.
