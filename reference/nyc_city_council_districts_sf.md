# New York City Council Districts

Simple feature collection of NYC City Council Districts. EPSG:2263,
NAD83 / New York Long Island (ftUS).

## Usage

``` r
nyc_city_council_districts_sf
```

## Format

### `nyc_city_council_districts_sf`

A simple feature collection with 51 rows and 4 columns:

- coun_dist:

  City Council district number

- shape_leng:

  Shape length

- shape_area:

  Shape area

- geometry:

  Multipolygon

## Source

<https://www.nyc.gov/content/planning/pages/resources#datasets>

## Details

Boundaries of NYC City Council Districts. Produced by NYC Planning
Department. Release: 25C, August 2025.
